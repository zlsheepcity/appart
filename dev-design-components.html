Self-Service Solution Perks

The Self-Service solution is between the White Label and Turnkey solution, taking longer to develop than the White Label solution but coming at a lower price than the Turnkey solution.

It supports all currencies, as well as an unlimited number of languages the operator can integrate. The solution also comes with numerous other benefits, such as the loyalty system, sports bonuses and free bets, live chat integration, CRM and automated mailing system, advanced reporting system, and integrated internal CMS system. Customisation Options

The Self-Service sportsbook solution allows clients to implement their own website design. However, there are many other customisation options available with the Self-Service solution, such as the possibility to customise the front-end, widgets, and change the colour scheme. In addition, all other additional functionalities can also be customised and implemented by clients’ developers.

Finally, operators will be able to rearrange blocks on a page freely and will be able to position custom-designed banners on their website. A few additional perks include full access to geolocation technologies, custom odds and lines, automatic live events configuration, as well as a cash-out option with an adjustable commission.
